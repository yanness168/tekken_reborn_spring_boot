Tekken Reborn!

Tekken 4 Reborn is an extensive overhaul of Tekken 7 to make it look, sound and feel like Tekken 4.

Main Features:

            - Tekken 4 Preset costumes for Kazuya, Jin, Steve, Xiaoyu, Jack, Heihachi and Marduk.
            - Tekken 4 warm up stage
            - Tekken 4 music included
            - Tekken 4 character portraits for 19 characters
            - Hon-Maru stage and the Inner Shrine BG music for the Mishima Dojo stage
            - Custom splash image
            - Custom startup logo screens
            - Replaced T7 intro with Tekken 4 intro
            - Title screen is now Jin doing his Karate practice from Tekken 4
            - Tekken 4 style character select
            - Tekken 4 style stage select
            - Tekken 4 style punishment training
            - Tekken 4 announcer and menu sounds
            - Tekken 4 Legacy voices for Law, Lee, Steve, Xiaoyu and Hwoarang
            - Tekken 4 loading and saving icon
            - Tekken 4 style main menu and online menus
            - Tekken 4 style default player panels (can still be changed in player customization)
            - Complete in game hud overhaul with antenna indicator, lifebars etc.
            - Tekken 4 style versus screen with reworked player stats (A rank S rank etc.)
